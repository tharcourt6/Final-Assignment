My Field Watch

Seiko Tortoise

According to Seiko's Website:

Prospex Land. Designed for those who treat life as the ultimate adventure, Seiko Prospex keeps moving forward, meeting every challenge with technological excellence and winning design. Recalling the styles and earth-tone colors of the 1970s, this newest collection is designed specifically for outdoor activities such as hiking, including a compass bezel and a listing of emergency codes on each screwdown caseback.

My Thoughts

This watch is a great piece from Seiko, as it is rare to find Seiko watches in this price category with a sapprhire crystal (as they tend to prefer their proprietory and inferior Hardlex crystal). The compass bezel adds a unique flair to it that you don't see on a lot of field watches, and adds a layer of practicality to this piece. Finally, that black and gold look is just mezmorizing! A truly magnificent piece
