My Dive Watch

Glycine Combat Sub

According to Glycine's Website:

In 1967, efforts were made to replicate the Airman's success in the world of aviation for the people of the land, spawning the Combat collection. As stated in the original manuals, this new Glycine collection is designed and manufactured for the military, sports industry, and all exacting men. Sporting a classic simple dial with superluminova indices, be ready to take on any challenge.

My Thoughts

Dive watches can start to look very same-y after a while, but this watch is very unique. Its incredibly dark colour palette with the pvd black coating on the case and bracelet, with the blue dial and bezerl and military style dial, make it look very unique. Furthermore, it is INCREDIBLY thin for a watch with 20bar of water resistance. A truly astonishing swiss made dive watch with no real competition in its proce bracket.
