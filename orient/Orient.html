My Dress Watch

Orient Sun and Moon v3

According to Orient's Website:

This classic ORIENT 42.5mm mechanical watch showcases a stainless steel case and blue crocodile textured band with a stylish blue dial. Complementing its sophisticated design are attractive white hands and roman numeral hour markers, individual apertures for date, day, and sun and moon tracking. A hand-wound crown with automatic self-winding functionality completes the watch's alluring design.
